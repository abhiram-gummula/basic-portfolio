Food Ordering App

"Street Bite" is a single-page food ordering application showcasing a config-driven UI. This application offers a seamless user experience with high-level features such as cart functionality, search capability, shimmer component for loading visuals, and lazy loading with fallback for optimal performance. By utilizing real data from Swiggy's public API, users can explore various restaurants and their food items in detail. The application leverages Tailwind for styling, Redux store for cart-related data, and react-testing-library for testing the header component. It is built from scratch without relying on create-react-app, incorporating simple and controlled accordions, react-router-dom for page navigation, custom hooks, and parcel as the bundler for optimal efficiency.
